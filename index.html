About Host Institutions

Loyola College, Vettavalam

Run by the Jesuits, Loyola College is committed to providing quality higher education to the rural youth. As an associate institution of Loyola College, Chennai, it focuses on holistic formation and academic excellence.

LISSOR

Loyola Institute of Science and Social Research (LISSOR) fosters a culture of inquiry and innovation, bridging the gap between scientific advancement and social reality through impactful research.

Ruth S. Ammon College

Adelphi University, New York, USA

The Ruth S. Ammon College of Education and Health Sciences prepares students to become changemakers, advocates, and champions for social justice through research, collaboration, and leadership. The School of Education offers distinguished undergraduate and graduate programs, while the School of Health Sciences provides comprehensive degrees in Communication Sciences and Disorders.

About the Conference

ICCE 2026 is a premier gathering of global minds dedicated to comprehensive education. Under the theme "Empowerment Beyond Barriers", we aim to bridge the gap between policy, practice, and innovation. The conference serves as a catalyst for creating inclusive environments that celebrate diversity and foster equitable learning opportunities for all.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, academicians, and practitioners to submit their original work for ICCE 2026. Join us in shaping the future of inclusive education through innovative research and dialogue.

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts must be relevant to the theme "Empowerment Beyond Barriers".
  • All submissions will undergo review by the committee.
  • Accepted papers will be presented during the conference sessions.
